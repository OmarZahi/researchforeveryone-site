Why host supervised sessions?

Risk controls

We operate only within BSL‑1 and basic digital fabrication tools. We enforce strict supervision ratios, follow your rules first, and maintain detailed incident reporting and named chaperones.

Insurance & paperwork

We handle certificate tracking, waivers and visitor badges, sign‑in/out logs and guardian consent forms so your staff can focus on science.

Economics

Choose from two revenue models: share the ticket revenue from supervised sessions or receive a fixed facility fee with consumables billed at cost.

Operational flow

Request to partner

Submit a short application so we can understand your facility, capacity and policies.

Alignment

We work with you to define the allowed equipment, supervision ratios and SOPs. Our Passport training aligns with your safety policies.

Pilot afternoons

Run a couple of off‑peak supervised sessions to validate the process, collect feedback and adjust as needed.

Review & scale

After pilots, we review incident logs, satisfaction and unit economics before expanding capacity or scope.
