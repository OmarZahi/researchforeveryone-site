Our mission is to enable the next generation of makers and scientists with practical, supervised lab time without compromising safety.

Mission

Research for Everyone exists to make hands‑on science accessible. We provide a clear pathway for students and early‑career researchers to get real laboratory experience through standardized safety training and supervised sessions at partner labs.

Approach

We align with each host lab’s standard operating procedures, enforce strict supervision ratios and access controls, and keep all activities within Biosafety Level‑1 and basic digital fabrication tools. By handling training, consent and logistics, we remove friction for hosts while ensuring a safe learning environment.

What We Offer

Through our Lab Readiness Passport and network of partner labs, we offer curated training, insured and supervised lab sessions, mentorship opportunities and a pathway to build a portfolio of real‑world skills.

Our values

Safety first

We never compromise on safety. Every participant is trained, supervised and insured.

Equity & access

We believe every curious mind deserves a chance to learn, regardless of background or affiliation.

Collaboration

We build bridges between institutions, disciplines and generations to foster innovation.

Roadmap

We’re currently preparing our first pilots in Cairo, focused on BSL‑1 and basic fab labs. In the coming year we plan to expand to more cities, onboard additional partner labs and introduce advanced instrument open days.
