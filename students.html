Complete the safety course to earn your Lab Readiness Passport. Then book insured, supervised sessions at partner labs.

Get the Passport Find a Session

Why this matters

Build your portfolio

Get real equipment access and document your work to showcase to future mentors and employers.

Demonstrate readiness

Show labs and supervisors that you understand safety protocols and are prepared to work in a real research environment.

Unlock opportunities

Create pathways to internships, research projects and collaborations by gaining hands‑on experience early.

Eligibility

Ages

High school students, undergraduates and recent graduates are welcome. Guardian consent is required for anyone under 18.

Experience

No prior lab experience is needed. Our Passport training will teach you everything you need to know to get started safely.

Commitment

Complete a 3–4 hour training and exam to earn your badge; supervised sessions typically run 1–3 hours each.

What you’ll do

3D printing & fabrication

Learn to operate printers, laser cutters and other basic fabrication tools under guidance and supervision.

Microscopy & demos

Observe prepared specimens and demonstrations using optical microscopes and other imaging equipment.

Electronics & soldering

Practice safe soldering techniques and build simple circuits in a controlled environment.

Instrument open days

Participate in operator‑run sessions on more advanced instruments; watch the process and learn.

Pricing

Passport

A one‑time fee covers the training, exam, badge and administration.

Sessions

Pay per supervised session; costs vary by length and equipment. Consumables are charged at cost.

Discounts

Schools and groups can purchase packs of Passport seats and session hours at reduced rates.

What to bring

PPE & clothing

Follow host lab policies for personal protective equipment. Wear long pants, closed shoes and tie back long hair.

Identification

Bring a government‑issued photo ID and, if under 18, a signed consent form from your guardian.

Enthusiasm

Come curious and ready to learn! We’ll provide all tools and materials for the session.

Outcomes

Valid badge

Your Lab Readiness Passport is valid for 12 months, subject to renewal and revocation if safety rules are broken.

Portfolio credit

Receive an attendance record and optional photos (with consent) to document your learning for future applications.

Pathway to projects

Use your Passport and training to step into internships, hackathons and other research opportunities.

Our partners

Institutions helping students get hands‑on access.
